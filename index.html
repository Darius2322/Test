📖 FAQs
🔑I forgot my password. What do I do?›
Contact your admin via WhatsApp or call Darius on 0110554040. Your admin can reset your password directly in the system.
📵What happens when I lose internet?›
The app works fully offline. Any status updates you save will be queued and automatically synced to the server as soon as your connection returns. You'll see a pending count in the top bar.
✅How do I mark my list as Finished?›
Go to your Call List screen. At the bottom bar, tap the green "✅ Finished" button. This notifies the admin that you have completed your assigned sheet. You can undo this if needed.
📊How do I export my data?›
Tap the "⬇️ Export" button in the bottom bar on your dashboard, or inside your Call List screen. This downloads an Excel file of your assigned contacts and their statuses.
🔄Why does my status say Altered?›
A contact is marked "Altered" when its call status was changed after it was already set once. This helps the admin track which records were updated multiple times.
📲Can I install this as an app on my phone?›
Yes! Look for the install banner at the bottom of the screen. Tap "Install App" and it will be added to your home screen as a standalone app with a splash screen. On iOS, use Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen.
